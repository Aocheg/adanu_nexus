Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about how we work across all four service lines.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes. Software and design projects are handled fully remotely. Nursing and mental health services depend on your location — contact us to check availability in your area.

How much do your services cost?

Pricing depends on project scope or care needs. We provide a clear quote after an initial consultation, with no hidden fees.

How long does a typical software or design project take?

Simple websites usually take 1–3 weeks; larger applications or full brand packages can take longer. You'll get a timeline before work begins.

Is nursing care available for short-term or long-term needs?

Both. We support one-off post-surgical recovery periods as well as ongoing long-term care arrangements.

Are mental health sessions confidential?

Yes, all sessions are confidential and handled by licensed practitioners, in line with standard professional and legal confidentiality requirements.

How do I get started?

Reach out through our contact page and let us know which service you're interested in — we'll schedule a consultation from there.
